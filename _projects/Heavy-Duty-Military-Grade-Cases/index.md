## Working with foam
I spent 2 years working with foam at UK form converter, during that time I started on the workshop floor running a number of computer numerical control (CNC) machines, learning about how they operated and how the materials we where working with reacted to the forces that they were subjected too during the machining process.

Wall thicknesses, Tooling marks, swarf wrapping around the tool, material lifting from the vacumn bed, delamination. All factors between success and failure
